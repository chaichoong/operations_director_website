Questions

Straight answers, no fluff.

What am I actually buying?
+
A subscription to a set of pre-built operating system modules. Each module is a working Airtable database, Make.com automations, AI workflows via Claude, Slack integrations, dashboards and SOPs — all pre-wired. You pay monthly for access, updates and support.
Do I need Airtable, Make.com or Slack already?
+
Yes — modules install into your own Airtable and Make.com accounts. If you don't have them, we'll walk you through setup. You'll need paid accounts on those platforms (their pricing is separate).
What happens if I cancel my subscription?
+
You keep everything installed. Every module lives inside your own Airtable and Make.com, not ours. When you cancel, module updates stop and support ends — but the operating system keeps running. No data hostage-taking.
Is my data safe?
+
Your data never touches our servers. It lives in your Airtable, your Make.com, your Slack. We build on platforms with enterprise-grade security. NDAs signed as standard.
How is this different from Notion or Airtable templates?
+
Templates give you a blank database and a manual. Operations Director ships full working systems — data model, automations, AI layer, SOPs and dashboards — all wired up. You don't configure. You use.
Can I upgrade or downgrade tiers?
+
Anytime, both directions. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle. Data and modules stay intact either way.
What size business is this built for?
+
Founder-led SMEs with 1–50 employees. Solo founders pick the Solo tier. Growing teams pick Team. 20+ person operations with complex needs pick Full OS. Over 50 employees, we're probably not the right fit.
Do I need technical skills?
+
No. Everything is built for non-technical operators. Your team uses Slack, fills Airtable forms, reads dashboards. The technical layer — Make.com scenarios, API integrations, AI prompts — runs in the background and is maintained by documented SOPs.
What if a module doesn't fit my business?
+
Every module covers 80% of the common case. For edge cases, modules are fully modifiable — by you, a freelancer, or us on retainer. You're never stuck with a system that doesn't fit.
Do you offer refunds?
+
Monthly billing means you can cancel anytime — so refunds rarely come up. If a module fundamentally doesn't work as described, we refund the current month. No long contracts, no exit fees.
Can you integrate with tools I already use?
+
Usually, yes. We integrate with Gmail, Google Workspace, GoHighLevel, Xero, QuickBooks, Stripe, HubSpot, Pipedrive and dozens of others via Make.com. If a tool has an API, we can connect it.
Why should I trust Operations Director?
+
Because every module ships from a real business running it daily — a multi-city property portfolio, a consultancy, and a charity brand. We don't sell theory. We sell what we use.
